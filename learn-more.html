Welcome to Royal Crown Ministries

Join us as we grow in faith, build a loving community, and proclaim the good news of Christ to the world. Together, we make a difference.

Men's Fellowship

Empowering men to lead in faith, family, and community. Through prayer, mentorship, and gatherings, we equip men to grow spiritually and positively impact their families.

Women's Fellowship

Connecting women to God and each other through prayer and service. Our fellowship provides a safe space for women to strengthen their faith and encourage one another.

Youth Ministry

Encouraging the next generation to live boldly for Christ. We provide programs, mentorship, and social activities to help young people stay connected to their faith.

Children's Church

Helping kids learn about God's love through fun and engaging activities. We provide age-appropriate lessons and activities to build a strong foundation in faith.

Choir and Band

Leading worship with inspiring music and heartfelt praise. Our choir and band bring together passionate individuals who use their talents to glorify God through music.

Ushering

Welcoming everyone with a smile and a helping hand. Our ushering team ensures a warm and organized worship experience for all attendees.

Multimedia

Spreading the message through creative visuals and media. Our team manages photography, video production, and live streaming to enhance our outreach.

Sound Crew

Ensuring crystal-clear audio for all our services and events. The sound crew is responsible for maintaining audio quality, ensuring an engaging worship experience.
